More About Village

Welcome to my village website — a window into the soul of a place I call home. Nestled in the heart of Nepal, our village is more than just landscapes and traditions; it’s a living story of resilience, warmth, and quiet beauty. I created this page to honor the people who shaped me, the paths I walked as a child, and the culture that continues to inspire me. Every photo, every word, is a tribute to the spirit of our community. Thank you for being part of this journey.

Festivals and Culture

Life in our village is filled with colors of festivals and celebrations. We celebrate Dashain and Tihar with great ethusiasm, where familes come together, elders bless the younger ones, and homes are decorated with lights and flowers, and the entire village resonates with joy and unity through food, music and dance. other festivals like holi,teej,and maghe sankrantu are also celebrated.

Temples and Spirituality

The village is home to several beautiful temples surrounded by nature. These temples are not just places of worships but also centers of community life. people gather here for prayers,festivals and important life event. Our main God is Masto and we have temples dedicated to him in the village and also we have temple foer other gods like shiva, Devi and Kalika.

Natural Beauty and joy

Beyond culture and Spirituality, The natural beauty of our village makes it truely special. Hills,Forests and fast flwing streams creates a picturesue landscapes that is both peaceful. the air is fresh and the scenery is breathtaking also you can Enjoy hunting.
