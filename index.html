Welcome To My village

My village named Layakada is quiet and beautiful place nestled in the hills of Nepal. Life here is simple, peaceful and deeply connected to nature the people are warm welcoming and our traditions are filled with meaning.

The village is surrounded by lush green feilds, terraced hillsides and dense forests. The air is fresh and the scenary is breathtaking.The pace of life is slow allowing one to savor. Agriculture is the main Occupation here with rice,maize and millet being the primary crops, During the evenings,children play freely in the open feilds while elders gather to share stories.

Livestock such as goats,cows and chicken are commonly reared providing sustenance and income for villager. We have a very close relationship with animals and they are an integral part of our daily lives. Cow is National animal and Oxes are highly valued for their role in farming.
